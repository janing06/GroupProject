About

Eros Apparel

Eros Apparel is named after Eros, A Greek god of love, or more precisely, passionate and physical desire. In Latin he is called Amor (love) or Cupid (desire). Eros was the assistant, and according to some the son, of Aprhodite, the goddess of love and fertility.

Because Eros represented love, our items will make everyone around you fall in love with you.
